Overview of TPC Sawgrass

This webpage is here to give you a closer look at the hosting course of The Players PGA Tournament: TPC Sawgrass

Fun Facts about TPC Sawgrass

  1. It is the home of the Players Championship
    • This tournament moved to TPC Sawgrass in 1982.
    • The tournament is played in March each year.
  2. Challenging Hole Design
    • TPC Sawgrass was designed to not favor one type of play style.
    • With holes that demand a safe and risky play style, the par 3's,4's, and 5's each offer different yardages so players can't get comfortable.
  3. Infamous Hole 17
    • The island green can either make or break your round at TPC Sawgrass.
    • From 2000-2016, only three hole-in-one's happened out of the 7,400 recorded attempts.
    • Hole 17 has claimed 983 balls since 2003 at The Players tournament.
